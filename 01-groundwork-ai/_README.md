# 01-groundwork-ai

GroundWork AI — your done-for-you AI ops + lead-gen business for small home-services contractors.

## subfolders

- **business-strategy/** — positioning, services, pricing, ICP, prospect audit process, assessment SOPs. Anything about *what GroundWork sells and how*.
- **corcontracting/** — the proof-of-concept client (Corey's NJ remodeling business). Live system documentation, not hypotheticals. Subfolders: `ghl/`, `airtable/`, `sops/`, `marketing/`.

## what does *not* go here

Personal notes (use `03-personal/`). HVAC sales coach work (use `02-hvac-sales-ai/`). Daily notes (use `daily-notes/`).
